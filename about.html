About SPARK Community

SPARK is a community for students of SKIT, JAIPUR. We aim to promote Collective intelligence. We’re helping each other to perfect our craft. We are a group of enthusiastic people ranging from cultural to technical experts willing to teach and make people thrive in community culture.

 

“None of US is as smart as all of US”

How SPARK started?

On October 23rd, 2018 SPARK became a reality. SPARK is a student council of SKIT whose idea was proposed in session that took place on Tuesday, 23rd Oct in JC Bose Seminar Hall. CS/IT 2nd year attended those sessions & they came forward to support the initiative. Now, we are turning to every college student to help us understand how they want to contribute!

What is the aim of SPARK?

We have simply 3 objectives-

  1. E3: Explore, Experiment & Evolve:

    2. By the means of community, you get to know about various fields/activities (be it technical or cultural) in which other people are engaged. Hence you can try each of those to identify whether they suits to your interest or not, thereby identify your passion.

  2. Get Support:

    3. Suppose that you want to organize an event or participate in a competition. The community will support you thoroughly from getting permission from College Management to helping you in executing your plans.

  3. No Categories:

    4. You are not restricted to be in only one domain out of Technical, Cultural and Career, they are only tags to refer the various activities collectively. SPARK is not a Club; it is community wherein you can switch to any domain you want, to learn anything you want!

How can I become a part of SPARK?

You are just a form away.

Who all can contribute to SPARK?

In this era of competition everyone is busy in getting the first place, competition with others and letting them down for putting yourself at first place.

This community is for everyone except those.

Everyone can contribute to SPARK. Coming together is a beginning. Keeping together is progress. Working together is success.

Who should I contact to be a part of SPARK?

No one. If you're enthusiastic and helpful or you want to be helped, you're free to ask any questions on the mailing list, WhatsApp group, or social accounts.

What I will get by joining any community?

Community gives you Exposure, Teachings, Regular Seminars, Talks, Projects to contribute and everything that you need to thrive in the era of smart skills. Community will give you everything, except the “done- nothing-but-got” certificates.

Run behind excellence, certificates will run behind you.
